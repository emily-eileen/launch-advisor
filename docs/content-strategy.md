## Content Funnel Architecture

The formation-to-banking funnel is the core content cluster:

```
[TOFU] How to start a business / LLC formation guide
    ↓
[TOFU] Do you need a business bank account for an LLC?
    ↓
[MOFU] What documents do you need to open a business bank account?
    ↓
[MOFU] Best business bank account for LLC (comparison)
    ↓
[BOFU] Mercury / Relay / Bluevine review (direct affiliate placement)
```

EIN article is the bridge between formation and banking:
```
How to Form an LLC
    → How to Get an EIN (required before banking)
        → How to Open a Business Bank Account
            → Best Business Bank Account for LLC
```
