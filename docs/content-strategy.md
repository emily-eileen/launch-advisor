## Content Funnel Architecture

The formation-to-banking funnel is the core content cluster:

```
[TOFU] How to start a business / LLC formation guide
    ↓
[TOFU] Do you need a business bank account for an LLC?
    ↓
[MOFU] What documents do you need to open a business bank account?
    ↓
[MOFU] Best business bank account for LLC (comparison)
    ↓
[BOFU] Mercury / Relay / Bluevine review (direct affiliate placement)
```

Each article links to the next stage, creating compound affiliate traffic.

## Internal Linking Strategy

Every article should link to:
1. The LaunchAdvisor checklist (sign up CTA)
2. At least 2 other articles in the cluster
3. 2–3 affiliate CTAs (contextual, not banner)

The checklist tool itself is a backlink magnet — reach out to:
- Small business blogs (ask them to cite your checklist)
- Accounting/bookkeeping blogs
- Local Small Business Development Centers (SBDCs)
- SCORE chapter websites
