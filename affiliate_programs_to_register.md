# LaunchAdvisor Core Affiliate Integrations

To properly monetize the high-intent paths established across the 30+ steps and programmatically generated SEO guides in LaunchAdvisor, you need to sign up for the following crucial affiliate networks. 

Each of these platforms represents a direct, highly-contextual monetization node built into our "Business Launch OS."

---

### 1. Legal & Formation Platforms (Phase 4: Form)
This is typically the highest converting category for new founders.
*   **ZenBusiness Affiliate / Partner Program**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 4.3 (Filing Formation Documents)
    *   *Why:* Fast, highly optimized conversion funnel. They offer substantial payouts per LLC formed.
*   **Northwest Registered Agent**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 4.3 and Step 4.2
    *   *Why:* Premium privacy-focused formation that many tech/service founders prefer over ZenBusiness.
*   **Bizee (formerly Incfile)**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 4.1 (Legal Structure Recommender)
    *   *Why:* Free LLC filing (just state fees) acts as a high-conversion lead magnet, monetized on the backend by Bizee.

### 2. Virtual Real Estate (Phase 4: Form Supplement)
If a founder registers a business with their home address, it enters the public record. 
*   **iPostal1 or PostNet Affiliate Programs**
    *   *Where it will be embedded:* New Step! "Setup a Virtual Mailbox"
    *   *Why:* Digital mail scanning is exploding. Provides a commercial business address needed for opening bank accounts + privacy.

### 3. Financials & Banking (Phase 5: Financials)
Once the EIN is printed, founders immediately need a business bank account.
*   **Mercury Partner Program**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 5.1 (Opening a Bank Account)
    *   *Why:* Top choice for SaaS, Tech, and E-commerce. Often pays $250+ per funded account.
*   **Novo or Relay Financial**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 5.1
    *   *Why:* Superior for Service/Consulting/Freelance users. Free business banking with excellent payouts.
*   **Bench Accounting or Keeper (Tax)**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 5.2 (Bookkeeping Setup)
    *   *Why:* Monthly recurring revenue share. Incredibly sticky product.

### 4. Branding & Visuals (Phase 6: Brand)
You want to capture users before they accidentally go down the rabbit hole.
*   **Fiverr Affiliate Program**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Throughout Phase 6 (Logo Generation, Copywriting) & "Do It For Me" Premium UI elements throughout.
    *   *Why:* Gigantic ecosystem. We will map "Premium AI Generator" to a highly vetted Fiverr Pro Service. You get a split of their first-time purchases.
*   **Looka or Canva Pro**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 6.1 (Logo Design)
    *   *Why:* AI-based logo design is instant. If they don't want Fiverr, they want Canva.

### 5. Website & Digital Operations (Phase 7: Web Setup)
Founders buying domains and hosting.
*   **Hostinger / Bluehost Affiliate Program**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 7.1 (Domain) and Step 7.2 (Website Builder)
    *   *Why:* Very high payouts. Hostinger is fast & cheap, great conversion.
*   **Google Workspace Referral Program**
    *   *Where it is embedded:* Step 7.3 (Professional Email)
    *   *Why:* Everyone needs `@yourcompany.com` instead of `@gmail.com`.

### 6. Merchandise & Communications (New Monetization Layers)
*   **OpenPhone or RingCentral Affiliate**
    *   *Where it will be embedded:* New Step! "Setup a Virtual Phone System"
    *   *Why:* Crucial so founders aren't using their personal iPhones for sales/support.
*   **StickerMule / Vistaprint / Printify**
    *   *Where it will be embedded:* New Step! "Order Foundational Promo Merch"
    *   *Why:* They have massive initial cart values (shirts, stickers, custom tape) for E-commerce & Food operators.

### Recommended Affiliate Aggregators
Instead of managing 15 separate logins, you can find a huge portion of these on these 3 centralized networks:
1. **Impact Radius** (Often has Canva, Hostinger, Envato)
2. **PartnerStack** (Goldmine for B2B tech like OpenPhone, Keeper, Webflow)
3. **ShareASale** (Usually carries Vistaprint, Legal services)
